Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

AI-Cabulary is designed to help users create and practice vocabulary cards on Apple devices.

Vocabulary entries, tags, and generated study content are stored locally on the user’s device. The app can optionally read text from user-selected images, camera captures, and pasted images to improve context for vocabulary analysis.

Vocabulary analysis runs on device. On first model preparation, the app may download model assets from third-party hosting sources such as Hugging Face or MLX Community in order to make local analysis available.

AI-Cabulary does not provide advertising, does not use tracking for cross-app or cross-site behavior, and is not intended to sell personal data.

Camera access is optional and only used when a user chooses to capture an image for OCR-based context extraction.

If you have privacy questions, contact jade@ornelas.io.
