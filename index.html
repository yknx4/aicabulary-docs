AI-Cabulary

On-device vocabulary practice for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Build study cards from words, phrases, typed context, and OCR from images. AI-Cabulary generates translations, explanations, and examples while keeping the learning flow local to your device.

Join TestFlight Support

What it does

AI-Cabulary helps language learners turn vocabulary into reusable study cards with translations, explanations, examples, tags, and lightweight practice.

How it works

The app warms a local model on supported Apple hardware, reads optional context from photos or camera images, and stores your vocabulary library on device.

Platforms

The current release plan targets iPhone, iPad, and Mac, with TestFlight distribution before App Store review.
